polyrhythm is a program for generating polyrhthms

The arguments are 'po:ly bpm', where bpm is the bpm of the quarter note.

'po:ly' is of the form 3:2, 5:3, etc. Unlike simple_and_odd.cpp or compound.cpp, these are not computed automatically, but hardcoded. The supported polyrhythms are:

  • 3:2
  • 4:3
  • 5:3
  • 7:3
  • 7:5

N.B. I cannot guarantee these are musically correct. My copout is that these are just examples, and if you wanted to do it better, libmetro would empower you to do so.
